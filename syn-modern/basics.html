EXTREME OWNERSHIP

We're all on the same team

Together in spirit of inquiry, as comrades, not adversaries.
Shared Goal - Right & best way to accomplish.
All viewpoints towards the goal are welcome.
No individual winner, Team wins.
Everyone is an equal participant. No hierarchy or special considerations.

All about facts, logic & topic at hand

Distingush between facts & opinions/interpretations.
Validity of assertions of fact, analyze the quality of evidence.
Debate one fact at a time.
Debate the idea.It is not about who cares more, who's loudest, who's most powerful or who's most articualte.

Don't make it personal

No name calling or personal attacks.
Never cast judgement on people. Value productivity measurement.
Assume everyone's intentions are good. Solidify assumptions through productivity.
Nobody loses face for changing their mind.
Reward people for carrying the group forward, rather than being right.

Be intellectually humble

Don't take things personally.
Listen to and respect every person and their viewpoint, even if you disagree.
Admit when you realize you're wrong, and cheerfully concede when others have good points.
Be curious. Even bad ideas can be useful. Can help us find new and better ideas.
Invest in your teammates - Take time to teach each other.
Brainstrom - Ideas are hard to generate in vaccum. We need other ideas to bounce off.
